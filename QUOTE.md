# FIRA Wellness Club Quote Notes

- Premium responsive wellness landing page.
- Sections for hero, benefits, classes, memberships, contact, and footer.
- Class catalog with Reformer Burn and Yoga y Soundbath.
- Live calendar page.
- Google Sheets-powered weekly schedule.
- Dynamic class availability.
- Reservation form.
- Stripe Checkout integration.
- Stripe webhook route.
- Stripe success-page fallback confirmation.
- Customer confirmation emails through Resend.
- Studio notification emails.
- Phone push notifications through ntfy.
- Vercel production deployment.
- Custom domain/live production setup.
- Multiple rounds of edits, bug fixes, and operational support.

## Recommended Monthly Maintenance

Recommended monthly retainer:

```text
$3,500 MXN / month
```

Reasonable range:

```text
$2,500-$4,000 MXN / month
```

Includes hosting management, domain/DNS checks, uptime checks, Stripe/webhook monitoring, Resend email monitoring, ntfy notification support, small text/class/schedule updates, up to 2 minor change requests per month, bug fixes for existing features, and monthly dependency/security checks.

Does not include new pages, new payment products/memberships, redesigns, full database/admin dashboard, marketing campaigns, SEO content writing, or paid ads setup.

## Platform Costs To Consider

- Vercel Pro: about $20 USD/user/month for professional/commercial use.
- Resend: free tier up to 3,000 emails/month, paid plans start around $20 USD/month.
- Stripe Mexico: 3.6% + $3 MXN per successful domestic card transaction.
- ntfy: works via public topic; topic secrecy matters because the topic acts like a password.
